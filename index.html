Instructions

The goal of this game is to kill all the monsters in the map.
To move, use WASD commands (W=forward, A=turn left, S=backward, D=turn right). To use the punch, press SPACE.
To change the difficulty, click the DIFFICULTY button (difficulty parameters are: number of monsters, velocity of the monsters and number of lives).
By clicking the OPTIONS button, you can choose the character and the time of the day in the environment.
You can also mute/unmute music by clicking the button on the top-right, while for turning on the lights and removing the fog you have to find the 'easter eggs' in the map (some 'strange' elements that have to be punched).
To change point of view use your mouse by clicking and scrolling in the opposite direction of what you want to see.
